What you'll find here

This website, Ecruvian.github.io, is my personal website. I use it to practice coding, store documents that I make for fun, and publish educational pieces. Look around to see if it looks like my style fits your needs. Every page and every document is made from scratch, unless I credit otherwise at the top.

Most of the individual articles were originally formatted in Markdown, often using LaTex for any maths or special formulas, then modified for the web. Most of the material I write benefits from being as clean and simple as possible, but you'll also find some resources that were made just for fun. I like to make simple PNG paintings and doodles to incorporate into my online profiles and printed documents!

Please enjoy my articles!
